Your personal Twitch assistant

Acolyte is a chat bot for Twitch that focuses on knowledge. He can look up things for you, answer your questions and much more.

Easy setup

You can use your existing Twitch account to authenticate with Acolyte. Once you have logged in you are one click away from having Acolyte joining your channel.

User friendly

Acolyte was designed to provide the best possbile user experience. He is extremely easy to use and there is absolutely no hassle when communicating with him.

Customizable

Enabling and disabling features in Acolyte can be done directly in your settings. We made these controls easily available so that you can change them whenever you feel like it.
